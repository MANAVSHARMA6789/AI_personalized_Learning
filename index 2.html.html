How SkillBridge Works

A five-stage NLP pipeline that transforms raw resumes into actionable career intelligence in seconds.

📄

Resume Ingestion

Upload PDF/DOCX. PyPDF2 & spaCy extract text, tokenize entities, and identify skills via NER.

🗂️

Job Role Mapping

Select target role. System loads curated skill taxonomy from 240+ structured job profiles.

🔍

Gap Analysis

Cosine similarity + NLP matching compares extracted skills against requirements by criticality.

🎓

Recommendations

Weighted engine suggests curated courses ranked by criticality, ROI, and time-to-complete.

📊

Analytics

Placement coordinators access real-time cohort analytics — skill trends, readiness scores.
